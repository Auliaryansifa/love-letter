Verses for You

In the depths of midnight's embrace, Where silence weaves its delicate lace, I find myself lost in thoughts of you— Like stardust scattered on morning dew. Each word I write becomes a star, Mapping constellations of who you are. In the poetry of your gentle grace, I've found my soul's resting place. Through seasons changing, time advancing, Like verses in eternal dancing, You remain my constant inspiration, Life's most beautiful constellation.
Forever in verses, [Aury]
